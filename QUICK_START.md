# Quick Start: 5 Minutes to Offline AI Voice

## The One-Page Guide for Busy People

### What This App Does
You speak → AI listens & responds → Everything stays private on your device (offline)

---

## Your First Time (One Setup Required)

### 1️⃣ Open App Link
Click the link your friend/doctor gave you
```
Example: https://your-app.vercel.app
```

### 2️⃣ Go to Voice Page
- Find "Voice" in the menu
- Click it

### 3️⃣ Download Models (Only Do This Once!)
You'll see three models. For each one:
1. Click the **"+ Download"** button
2. Wait for progress bar to reach 100%
3. Look for green checkmark ✓

**Models you're downloading:**
- Speech Recognition (800 MB) - Understands you
- AI Response (1.4 GB) - Thinks of replies  
- Voice Output (1.1 GB) - Reads to you
- **Total: 3.3 GB**

⏱️ **Takes:** 15-30 minutes on WiFi

⚠️ **Important:** Keep browser open while downloading. Don't close it!

### 4️⃣ You're Ready!
See this? → ✓ **"Ready for Offline Use"**

You can now close the browser. Models are saved on your device forever (until you delete them).

---

## Every Time You Use It

### Go to Voice Page
Click Voice in the menu

### Click "Start Recording"
- Browser asks permission to use mic
- Click "Allow"

### Speak Your Thoughts
- "I'm feeling stressed"
- "I had a good day"
- "I need to talk"
- Just talk naturally for 30 seconds - 2 minutes

### Click "Stop Recording"
Wait 3-5 seconds...

### Get Your Response
- See what emotion you had
- Read AI's helpful response
- Listen to audio reply

### Done!
Everything was processed on YOUR device. Private. Offline. Safe.

---

## After First Setup: One-Time Things

Shows how many GB models take (usually 3-4 GB)

### See Your Emotion Status
Shows:
- How you're feeling (happy, sad, anxious)
- Your stress level (0-100)
- How many times you've used app

✓ Private = Only on YOUR device, nobody sees it

❌ Don't close browser during download  
❌ Need 4GB space minimum

## If Something Goes Wrong

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Download is slow | Check WiFi, close other apps |
| Download stops | Refresh page, try again |
| Mic not working | Check browser settings, allow mic |
| App crashes | Refresh page, restart browser |
| Need more space | Delete old files, free up space |

## Questions?

**Do I need internet after download?**
No! Completely offline.

**Is my voice private?**
Yes! Stays on your device only.

**Can I delete models?**
Yes (click trash icon), but then you'll need internet for voice.

**Do I do this on every device?**
Yes, each phone/computer needs own copy.

**How many times can I use it?**
Unlimited times, forever!
