# Complete User Guide: Offline Voice Interaction

## Welcome to Your Private AI Assistant!

This guide explains how to set up and use your offline voice assistant - in the simplest possible terms.

---

## What Does "Offline" Mean?

**Simple explanation:** Once you download the AI models, everything happens on YOUR device. Your voice, your thoughts, everything stays private and doesn't go anywhere else. You don't need internet to use it.

Think of it like this:
- **Regular apps:** You speak → Your voice goes to the internet → Someone's server processes it → Result comes back
- **This app:** You speak → YOUR device processes it → Result appears (no internet needed!)

---

## Getting Started: 5 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Open the App
1. You'll receive a link to the app (like `https://your-app.vercel.app`)
2. Click the link and open it in your web browser
3. You'll see the home page

### Step 2: Find "Voice" Section
1. Look at the navigation menu (usually at the top or bottom)
2. Click on "Voice" or the voice icon
3. You'll see a page with a big "Download Models" button

### Step 3: Download the AI Models (One Time Only!)
1. Click the **"Download Models"** button
2. You'll see three models that will be downloaded:
   - **Speech Recognition Model** (~800 MB) - Understands what you say
   - **AI Response Model** (~1.4 GB) - Creates responses to your thoughts
   - **Voice Output Model** (~1.1 GB) - Reads responses back to you
3. **Important:** You only do this ONCE. It can take 15-30 minutes depending on your internet speed
4. Each model will have a progress bar. Wait for it to reach 100%
5. Once done, you'll see a green checkmark next to each model
6. You can now **close the browser - the models are saved on your device!**

### Step 4: Check Your Storage
After downloading:
- Check how much space was used (usually shows total GB)
- These models will stay on your device until you delete them
- Your phone/computer needs at least 4GB free space for all three models

### Step 5: Use Voice Interaction (Works Offline!)
Now you can:
1. Click "Start Recording"
2. Speak your thoughts, feelings, or anything on your mind
3. The app will:
   - Understand what you said
   - Analyze your emotions
   - Generate a helpful response
   - Read it back to you
4. **No internet needed!** Everything happens on your device

## Using Voice Interaction: Step by Step

### Starting a Session:

1. Go to **Voice** page
2. Click **"Start Recording"** (big microphone button)
3. Your device will ask permission to use the microphone
   - Click "Allow" or "OK"
   - This is normal and safe

### Speaking to the AI:

1. After clicking allow, you can now speak
2. Say anything on your mind:
   - "I'm feeling anxious about tomorrow"
   - "I had a great day today"
   - "I need someone to talk to"
   - Just talk naturally!
3. The app will show your words as text on the screen
4. Keep talking - it listens in real-time

### When You're Done:

1. Click **"Stop Recording"**
2. The app will:
   - Analyze your emotions
   - Generate a response
   - Read it back to you (with audio)
3. You can see:
   - Your transcript (what you said)
   - Emotion detected (happy, sad, anxious, etc.)
   - Stress level (0-100)
   - AI response (helpful advice)

### Review Your Session:

- See all your stats
- Look at emotion graphs
- Check your session history
- All data stays on YOUR device

## Troubleshooting

### Download is Slow

**Why:** Internet connection or server load
**What to do:**
1. Check your internet speed (open another website)
2. Try downloading on a WiFi network
3. Close other apps and tabs
4. Try downloading one model at a time
5. Wait - downloads can be slow, be patient!

### Download Stops/Fails

**Why:** Connection interrupted or browser crashed
**What to do:**
1. Close all other apps
2. Refresh the page (hit refresh button)
3. Start the download again
4. Make sure WiFi is stable
5. Try on a different WiFi network

### Microphone Not Working

**Why:** Browser permission denied
**What to do:**
1. Check if browser is allowed to use microphone
2. In browser settings, enable microphone
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
4. Restart the app
5. Restart your device

### Running Out of Storage

**Why:** Your device is full
**What to do:**
1. Delete old files you don't need
2. Clear browser cache
3. Delete old photos or videos
4. Check how much space you have left
5. If less than 4GB, you need more space

### Models Won't Download

**Why:** File size too large or connection problem
**What to do:**
1. Check internet is working
2. Try different WiFi network
3. Close other browser tabs
4. Restart your browser
5. Update your browser to latest version

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need internet after downloading models?
**A:** No! Once downloaded, everything works completely offline.

### Q: Can I delete the models?
**A:** Yes, click the trash icon next to each model, but then you'll need internet to use voice features.

### Q: What if I turn off my device?
**A:** Models stay saved! Everything is still there when you turn it back on.

### Q: Can other people see my voice recordings?
**A:** No! Everything stays on YOUR device. No one else can access it.

### Q: What browsers work?
**A:** Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox. Avoid Internet Explorer (very old).

### Q: Can I use this offline forever?
**A:** Yes! Once downloaded, models work forever offline unless you delete them.

### Q: How long does download take?
**A:** Typically 15-30 minutes on good WiFi. Slower internet = longer wait.

### Q: What if internet cuts during download?
**A:** No worries! Just start again. It's safe to try multiple times.

### Q: Do I need to download on my phone AND computer?
**A:** Yes, each device needs its own copy (they're stored separately).

### Q: Can I share models with friends?
**A:** No, each person needs to download them. It's a one-time process though.

- Stored in browser's local storage
- Survives browser restarts
- Survives device restarts
- Only deleted if you clear browser data
