# First-Time Setup (Do This Once - Takes 30 Minutes)

## What You're Doing

You're downloading AI models to your device so everything works without internet. After this, your voice assistant is always available, and your conversations stay 100% private.

---

## Step-By-Step Instructions for Users

### Step 1: Open the App
1. Click the link your doctor/helper gave you
2. Wait for the page to load (should show a home page)
3. Look good? Great!

### Step 2: Go to Voice Section
1. Look at the navigation (usually menu at top or bottom)
2. Find and click "Voice" 
3. You should see a section called "Setup Offline Mode"

### Step 3: Start Downloading Models
You'll see a screen like this:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  Setup Offline Mode             │
│                                 │
│  Download once, use forever     │
│  without internet               │
└─────────────────────────────────┘

Storage:    0 MB
Downloaded: 0/3 models
Status:     Setup

┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ ✓ Model 1: Speech Recognition   │
│   Size: 800 MB                  │
│   [+ Download]                  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘

┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ ✓ Model 2: AI Response          │
│   Size: 1.4 GB                  │
│   [+ Download]                  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘

┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ ✓ Model 3: Voice Output         │
│   Size: 1.1 GB                  │
│   [+ Download]                  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 4: Click First Model's "Download" Button
1. Click the **[+ Download]** button next to first model
2. You'll see a progress bar appear
3. It will say "Downloading..." with a percentage
4. This takes about 10 minutes for first model
5. Wait until it reaches 100%
6. You'll see a green checkmark ✓ and "Downloaded" button

### Step 5: Download Second Model
1. Click **[+ Download]** for second model
2. Watch the progress bar (takes about 15 minutes)
3. Wait for green checkmark ✓
4. Now you're halfway done!

### Step 6: Download Third Model
1. Click **[+ Download]** for third model
2. This one is the largest (takes about 15 minutes)
3. Watch the progress bar
4. Wait for final green checkmark ✓

### Step 7: Confirm You're Ready
After all three are downloaded, you should see:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ ✓ Ready for Offline Use!        │
│                                 │
│ All models downloaded.          │
│ Turn off WiFi - it still works! │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Congratulations! You're all set!**

## Now Use the Voice Feature

### Recording Your First Session

1. Go back to Voice page (if you left)
2. You should still see the downloaded models with green checkmarks
3. **Important:** Scroll down to find the **"Start Recording"** button
4. Click it (it looks like a microphone)

### Browser Permission
The app will ask:
```
"This site wants to use your microphone"
```
Click **"Allow"** or **"OK"**

This is normal and safe - the app needs access to your microphone.

### Speaking
1. Now you can speak freely
2. Talk for 30 seconds to 2 minutes
3. Say anything on your mind:
   - How you're feeling
   - What's worrying you
   - What made you happy
   - Anything!
4. The app shows your words on screen as you speak
5. It's learning what you're saying

### Stopping
1. When you're done talking, click **"Stop Recording"**
2. Wait 3-5 seconds...
3. The app will:
   - Analyze your emotions
   - Figure out your stress level
   - Generate a helpful response
   - Read it back to you

### Your Results
You'll see:
- **Transcript:** What you said (in text)
- **Emotion:** Happy? Sad? Anxious?
- **Stress Level:** 0-100 scale
- **Response:** What the AI thinks will help
- **Audio:** Listen to response being read

## Troubleshooting

### Download is Really Slow
**Why:** Slow internet connection
**What to do:**
- Make sure you're on WiFi (not mobile data)
- Close other apps and tabs
- Restart your device
- Try downloading at a different time

### Download Keeps Stopping
**Why:** Connection interrupted
**What to do:**
- Check internet is still working
- Refresh the page
- Try again
- Be patient - downloading takes time

### Microphone Says "Permission Denied"
**Why:** App not allowed to use microphone
**What to do:**
- Check browser settings
- Look for microphone permission
- Make sure it's turned ON
- Try different browser

### Voice Recording Doesn't Work
**Why:** Various issues
**What to do:**
- Try different browser
- Make sure microphone is allowed
- Check microphone isn't broken
- Restart your device

### Can't Download Models?
**Why:** Connection problem
**What to do:**
- Make sure WiFi is working
- Try on different WiFi network
- Wait for better connection
- Try later

### Running Out of Storage?
**Why:** Device is too full
**What to do:**
- Delete old files you don't need
- Delete old photos/videos
- Clear browser cache
- Upgrade device storage
